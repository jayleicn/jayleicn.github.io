Short Bio

I am a 4th-year undergrad from Yingcai Honors College at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, with the interest on Computer Vision, Deep learning and Natural Language Processing.

Currently, I am a Research Assistant working with Prof. Sinno Jialin Pan in the beautiful campus of Nanyang Technological University, where I focus on the research of computer vision and transfer learning. Before that, I spend a summer in the Computer Vision Lab of University of Manitoba as a Mitacs Globlink Intern, working with Prof. Yang Wang.

Here is my CV, I am looking for a PhD program in 2017 Fall.
